Revenue & AR Analytics Dashboard

A Python-based analytics platform that replaces manual Excel workflows with audit-ready pipelines.

The Problem: Tracking aging AR and recognizing revenue manually via Excel was prone to human error, lacked strict auditability, and delayed executive decision-making.

The Approach: Built a Python data pipeline utilizing Pandas and Matplotlib to ingest raw ERP data, systematically apply the 5-step IFRS 15 / ASC 606 model, and auto-generate clean visualizations.

The Impact: Delivered 6 real-time, SOX-compliant dashboards tracking $1.14M in AR, optimizing visibility and empowering cross-functional teams with instant, accurate insights.
